# Threat Modelling Notes

## What is Threat Modelling?
A process to **identify, analyze, and mitigate security risks** in applications.

---

## Why Threat Modelling?
- Identify threats early
- Reduce attack surface
- Improve system security design

---

## Key Steps
1. Identify assets (data, services)
2. Create architecture diagram
3. Identify threats
4. Assess risks
5. Mitigate threats

---

## STRIDE Model
- S → Spoofing
- T → Tampering
- R → Repudiation
- I → Information Disclosure
- D → Denial of Service
- E → Elevation of Privilege

---

## DFD (Data Flow Diagram)
- External entities
- Processes
- Data stores
- Data flow

---

## Tools
- OWASP Threat Dragon
- Microsoft Threat Modeling Tool

---

## Benefits
- Secure architecture
- Proactive risk management
- Compliance readiness
